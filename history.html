HISTORY

The word BAGWIS means wings in English. We wanted to use the word Bagwis because it symbolizes freedom, and we wanted to let our artist have their freedom especially in expressing their works. That’s why the logo is a wing, because we wanted you to have your own wing/s and just be free. We also want to let you know that we are with you. We realized that there are people who is scared that’s why they never post their artworks, and it’s one of the reasons why we made this project. We wish to collab with you soon! We will wait for you <3
