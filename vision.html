VISION

Provide a safe space for artists and supporters to post their artworks freely and express their opinions and suggestions. One of the website's highlights is the appreciation tab; they can leave messages for their co-artists, supporters, or anyone they want to; note that it needs approval because the project's goal is to spread awareness, not harmful comments.
